Discover the Films of Studio Ghibli

Founded in 1985, Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation film studio that has produced several highly acclaimed feature films.

Of recent note, famed director and animator Hayao Miyazaki has reportedly come out of retirement to direct the first feature-length film for Studio Ghibli in several years. It has a tentative release date set for 2020.
